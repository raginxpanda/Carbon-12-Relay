RELAY 0.2.16 — "recent" / catalog now show genuinely newest first
=================================================================
Only electron/index.html changed. Pairs with BOT r264. Drag in -> Replace -> Yes.

THE BUG: the "Recent" strip and the blueprint catalog weren't showing your newest
blueprints first — new ones got buried.

ROOT CAUSE: sorting was by earned_at date, but most blueprints have NO backfilled
earned_at (only re-running catch-up fills real dates). Null dates collapsed to ""
and fell back to ALPHABETICAL — so genuinely-new blueprints scattered into the
alphabet instead of sitting at the top.

FIX (both sides):
 • BOT r264 — "recent" now sorts by insertion order (the auto-increment id), which
   is the true "most recently added" signal regardless of earned_at. Also sends id
   on each catalog item.
 • RELAY (this) — the catalog modal sorts by id DESC too (then date, then name),
   matching the bot. Newest additions always on top.

DEPLOY BOTH: bot r264 (no migration, just restart) + this index.html.
